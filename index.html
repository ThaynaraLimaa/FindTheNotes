How to use "Find The Notes":

The goal is to help you memorize all the notes on your guitar! Follow these steps to a better practice:

  1. Take your guitar
  2. Below, choose whether you want to practice with all the notes (natural and sharp notes) or only the natural notes.
  3. Click on the "Start" button to begin the challenge.
  4. When you click "Start," a musical note will be displayed (for example, A, B, C, etc.). You must findthat note on all 6 strings of your instrument.
  5. After finding all the notes, click "Next" to advance to the next note and repeat the process until you find all 7 or 12 notes on each string.
  6. In the end, you'll see how much time it took to find all the notes on the instrument's neck.
  7. To improve, you can try to beat your own record until you can find all the notes effortlessly!

So, are you ready to practice? Let's go!
